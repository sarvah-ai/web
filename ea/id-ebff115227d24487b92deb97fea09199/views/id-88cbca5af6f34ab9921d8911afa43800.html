Wisdom Roles ()
The Wisdom Roles View is an ArchiMate Business Layer view that visualizes the seven business roles within Sarvah’s Wisdom Layer, grouped under the Wisdom Council Collaboration. This view highlights how each role contributes to the collaborative ecosystem, their interactions, and their service to the External User through the Identity Quest Service. The roles are depicted in a swimlane-like structure, emphasizing their distinct responsibilities, dependencies, and checks, with the Wise Guide acting as the user-facing orchestrator. ArchiMate Elements in the Wisdom Roles View: Business Roles: Interpreter, Context Keeper, Skeptic, Narrator, Mediator, Ethos Guardian, Wise Guide. Business Collaboration: Wisdom Council Collaboration, aggregating all roles. Business Actor: External User (initiates queries), External Expert (for augmentation). Business Service: Identity Quest Service, realized by the Wise Guide and supported by other roles. Business Objects: User Intent, Cultural Artifact, Truth Circle, Quest Connection, Provenance Citation, Contested Narrative.

Context Keeper Purpose: Maintains and provides access to cultural memory, serving as the repository of timelines, relationships, and artifact contexts to anchor responses in historical depth. Responsibilities: - Stores and organizes cultural data (e.g., artifact typologies, historical timelines, relational links). - Provides facts and contexts to the Interpreter (for intent) and Narrator (for storytelling). - Maps direct relations (e.g., artifact to artist) and broader cultural constellations. - Ensures data is verifiable, supporting provenance citations. Dependencies: Informs the Interpreter and Narrator; relies on the Mediator to prioritize relevant data. Checks/Moderation: Moderated by the Mediator for relevance; validated by the Skeptic for accuracy. Alignment: - Curator View: Acts as the backbone of preservation, ensuring responses are grounded in vetted, primary sources. - Identity Engine View: Supplies the factual layers of truth circles, enabling the Wise Guide to connect users to broader narratives.
